xYAF: A Metric for Evaluating Punt Returns

More Info

As part of the 2022 NFL Big Data Bowl two classmates and I worked on developing a metric focused on special teams plays we named xYAF, short for expected yards after fielding. Using linear regression, logistic regression, and LASSO regression we developed xYAF with the goal of quantifying the effectiveness of punt returners against a neutral baseline, as well as examine the circumstances of a specific return.
